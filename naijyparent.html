🗣️
Talk to your child daily
Even 10 minutes of focused conversation daily builds trust and prevents rebellion in teenage years.
📵
Phone-free dinner
No phones at the dinner table. This one rule builds family bonds that last a lifetime.
🙏
Pray with your children
Children who pray with their parents carry faith into adulthood. Start when they are young.
📚
Read together weekly
Even 20 minutes of reading together per week develops vocabulary, intelligence, and focus.
🎯
Discipline with love
Correct the behaviour, never attack the child's character. Say "what you did was wrong" not "you are bad."
💰
Teach money early
Give children small amounts to manage from age 6. Money wisdom learned early lasts forever.
🤝
Involve extended family
In Nigeria, it truly takes a village. Grandparents, uncles, aunties — they are resources, not interference.
⚽
Let children play
Unstructured outdoor play develops creativity, social skills, and resilience. Don't over-schedule.
