Save Before You Spend
Pay yourself first. When salary enters, move 10–20% to savings immediately. Then live on what remains.
📱
Avoid Airtime Loans
Airtime advance and instant loan apps charge 30–300% interest annually. Avoid them. Build an emergency fund instead.
🏦
Diversify Your Income
One source of income is one disaster away from poverty. Every Nigerian should have at least two income sources.
⚠️
If It Sounds Too Good
No legitimate investment gives 50% monthly returns. If someone promises this, they are stealing your money. Run.
🏠
Rent vs Buy
In Nigeria, buying property outright is better than a mortgage in most cases. High interest rates make mortgages expensive.
📊
Track Every Naira
Write down every expense for one month. You will be shocked where your money goes. Awareness is the first step to control.
