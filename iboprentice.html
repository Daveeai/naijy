🤝 How Imu Ahia Works — The 4 Stages

1
Find Your Oga
A young person joins a master trader's business to learn the trade from the inside
2
Learn Everything
The apprentice works and learns — buying, selling, suppliers, customers, pricing, profit
3
Ime Ego (Settlement)
After 3–7 years, the master gives the apprentice capital to start their own business
4
Become an Oga
The former apprentice grows, then takes their own apprentices — the cycle continues
