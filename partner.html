What is Naijy?

Naijy is Nigeria's national digital infrastructure. It is a network of 110 platforms, each focused on a key sector of the country — from health and education to security, business, and governance.

Each platform is designed to connect citizens, businesses, and government in one place. Some platforms are already live. Others are being built. Together, they form one connected national system for Nigeria.

Naijy is not just an app. It is a movement to digitise Nigeria, sector by sector — and you can be part of it from the very beginning.
