About Naijy

Naijy is Nigeria's national digital infrastructure. It's a network of structured platforms designed to organize key sectors, support citizens, and strengthen collaboration across the country. Each platform focuses on a specific area — including youth, education, business, health, security, and more. Some platforms are already live, while others are in development, all forming one connected ecosystem.

Explore Naijy Platforms

How Naijy Is Structured

Naijy is organised into a network of connected platforms. Each platform focuses on an important sector of Nigeria.

  • Categories – The main sectors such as youth, education, business, health, and more.
  • Special Platforms – Key systems that power the Naijy ecosystem.
  • Strategic Platforms – Platforms designed for national coordination and long-term impact.
