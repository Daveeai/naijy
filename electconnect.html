Register to Vote
Visit any INEC office or use the INEC online portal to register. Registration is free and mandatory to vote in 2027.

Check Your PVC
Your Permanent Voter Card (PVC) is your most powerful document in 2027. Collect it from your INEC office if you have not.
🗳️
Know Your Polling Unit
Visit voters.inec.gov.ng to find your polling unit. Arrive early on election day — polls open at 8:30am.
📵
Do Not Sell Your Vote
Vote buying is a crime. The person offering you ₦2,000 for your vote will steal billions if elected. Your vote is worth more.
📱
Report on Election Day
If you see anything suspicious, report it here on ElectConnect immediately. Take a photo or video if safe to do so.

Your Vote is Secret
No one can see how you voted. Do not be intimidated. Vote your conscience — it is protected by law.
